Steve Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, in San Fransisco, California. His birth parents put him up for adoption, and he was adopted by Clara and Paul Jobs. During his childhood, Steve jobs lived in Mountain View, California. In high school, Steve Jobs was introduced to his future business partner, Steve Wozniak. After high school, Jobs enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon. Six months later, he dropped out. Steve got a job as a video game designer with Atari, but eventually resigned to take a trip to India. After his trip, in 1976, Jobs and Wozniak began the first version of the Apple Computer in Steve's garage.
